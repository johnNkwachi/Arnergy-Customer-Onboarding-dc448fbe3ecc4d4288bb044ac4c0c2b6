Step 4: Credit Check

After agreeing to the terms of the lease agreement, you'll move on to the "Credit Check" stage.

  • Carefully enter your BVN in the provided field. Ensure that you input it correctly to avoid any errors.

This information is used to assess your creditworthiness and determine if you meet the eligibility criteria for the lease. The BVN entry is a standard security practice to verify your identity and assess your financial standing.

  • After entering your BVN, you may need to click a "Check" button or a similar action to initiate the credit check process.

  • If you're eligible based on the credit check, you'll proceed to the next stage of the onboarding process.
