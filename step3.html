🔋 Arnergy Customer Onboarding

Step 3: Agreement

After reviewing the "Payment Summary" and being informed about the payment structure, you'll proceed to the "Agreement" step.

  • On this page, you'll find the terms and conditions of the lease agreement that you're about to enter into.
  • Look for an "Agreement Icon" or "Agree" button. This icon/button typically represents your acceptance and agreement to the terms outlined.
  • By clicking on the "Agreement Icon" or "Agree" button, you confirm that you've read and understood the terms presented in the agreement.
  • Without clicking this icon/button, you won't be able to proceed to the next stage of the onboarding process.
  • Make sure to read the agreement carefully before proceeding, as this legally binds you to the terms specified.

Once you've clicked the "Agreement Icon" or "Agree" button, you'll be able to move forward to the next stage of the onboarding process. This step ensures that you're fully aware of and compliant with the lease terms, enhancing transparency and establishing a clear understanding between you and Arnergy.
